About Butterbean Bakery

Butterbean Bakery started from a high school baking class where the founder discovered a love for baking and sharing sweets with others. What began as baking at home for family and friends quickly turned into something bigger.

At just 16 years old, the idea for Butterbean began to grow. By age 20, the goal became turning that passion into a real bakery business.

Butterbean Bakery focuses on unique cookies and desserts with bold flavors like Cookie Monster, Cookie Dough Cupcakes, and Biscoff cookies.

The name Butterbean comes from the playful use of two B's — giving the brand a fun and memorable identity.
